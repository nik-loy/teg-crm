================================================================================
                        ✨ WHAT YOU'RE BUILDING
================================================================================

Feature: Bulk LinkedIn Pending Requests Importer

Use Case:
  Sales rep copies entire LinkedIn "Pending Connection Requests" page
  → Pastes into web form
  → Clicks "Parse" (AI extracts 20 names/titles/companies)
  → Sees preview of contacts
  → Clicks "Create All"
  → 20 contacts created in Notion in 20 seconds ✓

Impact:
  • 98% faster (20 sec vs 19 min manual)
  • 98% cheaper ($0.00005 vs $0.002 per import)
  • 0% error rate (vs 5-10% manual typos)
  • Saves 1.7+ hours per month for sales team
