Install the +TEG Bookmarklet

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

+TEG
  1. Open any LinkedIn profile
  2. Click +TEG in your bookmarks bar
  3. The add contact form opens pre-filled
On mobile: LinkedIn doesn't allow bookmarklets. Capture pending-request screenshots from the Sent Invitations page instead and use the Screenshots tab.
The bookmarklet runs entirely in your browser and sends nothing to external servers.
